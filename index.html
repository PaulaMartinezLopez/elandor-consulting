Profile

Fractional CFO services for pharma and manufacturing

I work with companies that need senior financial leadership without the fixed cost of a full-time CFO. I act as a fractional CFO in Madrid supporting management teams with reporting, control and decision-making.

My role is not limited to analysis: I build the financial structure management needs to operate, from reporting systems to internal processes and decision frameworks.

My background spans pharma, manufacturing, international trading and cross-border operations. I also design financial reporting systems connected to ERP and Power BI to give management real visibility.

What I do

Finance systems that serve the business

Management Reporting & Dashboards
One version of the numbers. Always ready. No manual intervention.

I design management reporting systems connected to ERP or Excel so leadership gets the information it needs without waiting on finance to build it manually every month.
Operational Visibility
Finance across the whole business, not just the P&L.
